We Are Nocturnal Syndicate!


Nocturnal Syndicate is a start-up game development company, based in Viborg, Denmark. The company is launching its first original game, The Case of Charles Dexter Ward, a co-op detective story. The game is an adaptation of the novel by the same name, written by the world famous author H. P. Lovecraft, creator of the Cthulhu Mythos.
